Extended Team Model

Increase your sprint output, shorten your time-to-market and lower your development costs with an Extended Team. We help you in recruiting, supporting and retaining a fully dedicated remote team of best-in-class software talent. This enables you to focus on your top priorities, tackle the roadmap and remain competitive. We thoroughly assess candidates, based on requirements we defined together, to make sure you only interview top-tier candidates suited for your organisation. After you have made your selection, your full-time dedicated team is ready to start. You manage your team directly, allowing for maximum control and quality. We support you by taking care of logistics, employee retention and continuous administrative support.

Dedicated recruitment

In this stage, we get to know each other. In an interview, it will be clear what your company does and looks like, what the core of the application is and what target group(s) and objectives are involved. In the requirement analysis, we establish global outlines, so that we can focus on the parts that provide you with the most value. Based on the requirement analysis, we select the appropriate project discovery program, create an action plan for the project discovery and, if desired, sign a non disclosure agreement, so that you are sure that your idea remains your own.
